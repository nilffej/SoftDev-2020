America's Leading Causes of Death in NYC

The National Center for Health Statistics at the CDC lists these ten causes of death below as the Leading Causes of Death in the United States.

This animated pie chart visualizes the change in proportion of deaths by these causes in New York from 1999 to 2017.
The whole represents only the deaths that these causes are responsible for and they exclude all other causes of death.
