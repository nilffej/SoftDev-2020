The contents below are held in a class container.
Bootstrap offers two containers types: container and container-fluid.
Container alters the size and spacing of divs based on viewport.
Container-fluid always uses the maximum space available on viewport.
A stand out feature of Bootstrap is its grid layout.
It utilizes classes row and col to designate spacing for divs.
There are also subclasses of row and col that indicate behavior depending on viewport size.
